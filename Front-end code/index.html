About Us

HOSTELGATE

Located in the Old Town of Vilnius right next to the Gate of Dawn, Hostelgate is the perfect place to enjoy your stay in Vilnius. Within a short walk you can explore all the sights, museums and churches of the Old Town. We offer accommodation for every need and our rooms range from 3-bed privates to 12-bed dormitories. You can also relax in our big and cozy common area, enjoy free coffee or tea in the kitchen, enjoy the summer sun on our balcony or warm up at our hostels sauna. Free on-site parking is provided as well. Reception is open 24 hours a day and our staff is speaking a lot of different languages so we are always ready to give you many useful tips and information about the city from cultural and historical sightseeing points, to local corners and places to find real Lithuanian food and drinks.

HOSTELGATE BnB

Hostelgate B&B is a very cozy and homely accommodation offering more privacy and is situated right in the heart of Vilnius Old Town. Rooms are spacious and bright, tastefully decorated and offer all modern conveniences. You definitely find your favourite space here with accommodation ranging from 4-Bed rooms to Double-Bed large rooms with private bathroom and a balcony to enjoy your morning coffee. All rooms have heating during the autumn and winter season. We offer you facilities like private keys, bed liners, towels, little tables and place to put your things as well as nice decoration details to feel welcomed. Free Breakfast is also offered and you will find a fully equipped kitchen, free tea and coffee, free Wi-Fi and BBQ equipment. Free on-site parking is provided as well and you can always relax in our elegant private yard.


Facilities

Both Hostelgate & Hostelgate BnB offer a big variety of accommodation options that you can choose from. You will also find a fully equipped kitchen, a cosy common area, laundry services and even free parking!!!
