Facilities

Hostelgate is the ideal place for backpackers, young travelers and people that want a cozy accommodation combined with a friendly and socializing atmosphere. It is located in the Old town, next to Gate of Dawn, in a walking distance to all the main attractions, bars and clubs that Vilnius has to offer. Prices depend on season/time so make sure you check them out through the booking system. Reception is open 24hours a day and we have the most amazing staff that is always willing to help you.

The Way to Hostelgate

~ From the Airport (5km) ~

BY TAXI

10-15 €
10-15 minutes
You can find a lot of Taxis in the aiport. Make sure you ask about the price before you go in!!!

BY BUS

1 €
20-30 minutes
Take bus 1 or 2 and get off at the last stop (Stotis-the train station). First bus is around 5:30 and last bus around 22:15.

BY TRAIN

1 €
15-25 minutes
Train station is out of the airport. Trains come every 30 minutes. Get off at last stop (Stotis).


~ From the Train station - Stotis (750m) ~

BY FEET

0 €       7 minutes
Once you are in the train station (Stotis) all you have to do is walk. Hostelgate is only 7 minutes away. Make sure you follow the instructions of the PDF.
